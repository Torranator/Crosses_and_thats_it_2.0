    Nike is an American multinational company specializing in sportswear and footwear. The headquarters is in the city of Beaverton (Oregon). Almost all Nike products are manufactured by third-party contractors outside the United States (mainly in Asia), the company itself is the copyright holder of trademarks, develops product design and owns a chain of stores (about 1,150 worldwide), as well as NikeTown shopping centers. Since September 20, 2013, it has been included in the Dow Jones Industrial Index. Nike is the most expensive sports brand in the world (according to Forbes). The company, founded on January 25, 1964 under the name Blue Ribbon Sports, officially became Nike, Inc. in 1978. Nike sells its products under its own brand, as well as under the brands Nike Golf, Nike Pro, Nike +, Air Jordan, Nike Blazers, Air Force 1, Nike Dunk, Air Max, Foamposite, Nike Skateboarding, Nike CR7, Hurley International, Converse. Nike sponsors many athletes and sports teams around the world. Since the 1990s, the company has been regularly criticized for the fact that its products are manufactured in factories where labor laws are violated.
